Lotus State is a music producer who creates ambient electronic tracks with dreamy, percussive elements. Having lived all over the world, Lotus State draws inspiration from a wide range of human experiences, blending them into a unique sound. The lotus flower symbolizes purity, resilience, and rebirth, which are themes that resonate deeply in Lotus State's music.

His work invites listeners into a 'lotus state,' a dreamlike experience where the lines between reality and imagination blur. While primarily focused on ambient music, Lotus State also explores various genres, including rap and phonk, and makes heavy use of samples. This mix of influences and styles gives his music an eclectic, yet grounded, feel that draws listeners into a calming, introspective dreamscape.
